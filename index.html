The Linear - Quadratic Model

    It includes the following main ideas:
  • The LQ is currently a standard model in work describing the effects of radioactivity on tissue response and cell survival.
  • The LQ model can be used in changing treatment regimens that are biologically equivalent, calculating compensation for interruptions in radiotherapy, and combining treatment modalities (X-rays, protons).
  • Expression form of The LQ is described by cell survival (S)(where α and β are the LQ parameters):
    • [LQ SF] = e α D + β D 2
  • The survived cells are calculated after n fractions and dose of each fraction d
    • [ LQ S ⁡ ( d ) ] n = e - n ⁡ ( α d + β d 2 )
